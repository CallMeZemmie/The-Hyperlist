Submission Rules

Level Standards

  • Moderators check decoration, gameplay, and overall quality.
  • Layouts are accepted, but gameplay must be original.
  • Copying levels results in warnings or bans.

CBF and Hacks

  • CBF (Click Between Frames) is allowed.
  • Hacks such as noclip or hitbox mods are forbidden.

Consequences

  • First Offense: Warning.
  • Second Offense: Temporary ban.
  • Severe Violations: Permanent ban.
